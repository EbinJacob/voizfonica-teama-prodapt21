FAQs

How to recharge my account?

After logging in to your account, head over ot the recharge panel.

How long does it take for the recharge to reflect in my account?

Try logging out and re-login. In most cases the recharge happens almost instantly. In some cases it might take maximum upto 24 hours.

What should I do in case the servers are down?

Our servers should be up within short durations. However it is adviced that the second payment attempt is made after a few hours.

What should I do if my issue stands active and I dont receive help?

We will try our best to resolve all issues as soon as possible. In case you are not satisfied with the response time, you can connect with our support team through the alternate contact.
